The inspiration for Circle came from an observation made by the British Anthropologist Robin Dunbar in the 1990s. He noticed that the bigger an animal's brain, the bigger it's social group. After crunching some numbers, he made a prediction : Humans are capable of having a maximum 150 meaningful social relationships at any given time. Since then studies have been done proving the precision of the now famous "Dunbar's Number". Don't believe me??? Let me google that for you
